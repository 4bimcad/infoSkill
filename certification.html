Certification

At InfoSkill.Work, we understand the importance of professional training and recognized certification.

Course Certification Process

Academic Requirements:

To qualify for certification, learners must successfully complete all required course modules and study materials. This ensures that you acquire and refine essential skills in a structured and effective learning environment.

Competency Assessment:

After completing the training, you will take a final assessment designed to measure your understanding and ability to apply the course content in real-world situations. For most courses, a passing score requires answering at least 18 out of 20 questions correctly.

Certificate Format:

Once your test results are verified and your certification fee is processed, you will receive a professionally designed certificate in A5 PDF Format. Each certificate includes a unique QR code for instant verification on our website. By scanning the code, employers or third parties can confirm your achievement and even download the certificate directly from our platform.

Processing Time:

Certificates are typically issued within 20 minutes to 2 hours after payment and verification of your exam results, depending on the course requirements.

Benefits of Certification

Professional certifications are your passport to advancing in regulated industries. They demonstrate:

  • Mastery of the latest industry standards and regulations
  • Ability to implement effective safety protocols
  • Readiness for audits, inspections, and compliance checks
  • Verified technical competence, increasing your professional credibility
  • Enhanced career opportunities in a competitive job market

In today’s demanding workplace, these credentials set you apart, ensuring you meet the highest standards of quality and compliance.

Course Price

All OSHA and EU OSH courses and training materials are provided Completely FREE of charge. You are welcome to download, share, and copy them without any restrictions — we fully support safe work practices worldwide.

You only pay the certification processing fee, which covers the issuance and validation of your official certificate.

Pricing & Payment

Certification fees may vary depending on the course and verification requirements.
Payment is processed securely via PayPal.

Important: Please ensure your payment includes your full name or the email address provided upon completion of the course test. This ensures accurate and timely delivery of your certificate.

Delivery

Certificates are Sent via Email to the address provided when completing the course test.
Please note: Depending on your email provider, our message may appear in your "Spam" or "Junk" folder — be sure to check these folders if you don’t see your certificate in your inbox.
