OSHA 10-Hour Construction Training Course

Comprehensive safety training to help construction workers understand OSHA standards and improve workplace safety.

Course Description

The OSHA 10-Hour Construction Training Course provides essential safety training for workers in the construction industry.

This course covers fundamental topics such as hazard recognition, fall protection, personal protective equipment, electrical safety, and safe work practices. Designed to promote workplace safety awareness and compliance with OSHA regulations, it equips participants with practical knowledge to prevent accidents and injuries on construction sites.

Course Objectives

  • Understand OSHA regulations and safety standards for construction.
  • Identify common construction site hazards and how to prevent them.
  • Learn proper use of personal protective equipment (PPE).
  • Gain knowledge of fall protection and electrical safety measures.
  • Promote a culture of safety to reduce workplace accidents.
  • Prepare for OSHA compliance and workplace inspections.

Compliance with Regulations and Legislation

  • Occupational Safety and Health Act (OSH Act) of 1970
  • OSHA Construction Standards (29 CFR Part 1926)
  • OSHA General Duty Clause, Section 5(a)(1)
  • OSHA Focus Four Hazards guidelines
  • OSHA Personal Protective Equipment standards (29 CFR Part 1910 Subpart I)
  • OSHA Excavations, Stairways and Ladders, Scaffolds regulations

Course Program

  • Module 1: Introduction to OSHA and Construction Safety
  • Module 2: Top Four Hazards in Construction
  • Module 3: Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Basics
  • Module 4: Health Risks and Exposure on Construction Sites
  • Module 5: Safe Use of Stairways and Ladders
  • Module 6: Operating Cranes, Hoists, and Elevators Safely
  • Module 7: Excavation Safety and Procedures
  • Module 8: Materials Handling and Waste Management
  • Module 9: Scaffold Safety and Inspections
  • Module 10: Use of Hand and Power Tools

Duration

This course has a duration equivalent to 10 academic hours.
